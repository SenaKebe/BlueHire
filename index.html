About us

I grew up seeing skilled workers like electricians, metal workers, and carpenters struggling to find consistent work — not because they lacked skill, but because no platform truly showcased their talent. Unlike office jobs, most tradespeople rely on word-of-mouth or roadside ads. I realized existing hiring apps weren’t built for them — they didn’t support visual portfolios, license verification, or simple direct hiring. That’s why I started ForgeHire: to help skilled hands be seen, hired, and respected.

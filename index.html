About us

I grew up seeing skilled workers like electricians, metal workers, and carpenters struggling to find consistent work — not because they lacked skill, but because no platform truly showcased their talent. Unlike office jobs, most tradespeople rely on word-of-mouth or roadside ads. I realized existing hiring apps weren’t built for them — they didn’t support visual portfolios, license verification, or simple direct hiring. That’s why I started ForgeHire: to help skilled hands be seen, hired, and respected.

Key Features

Worker of the Month Rewards

Recognizes the top-rated worker each month with a special prize, boosting motivation, trust, and service quality through public recognition and user ratings.

Voice-Based Profile Creation

Lets workers create profiles using voice only—no reading or typing needed. It’s fast, easy, and accessible for everyone, especially those with low literacy.

Smart Job Matching

Instantly connects employers with the right workers based on job descriptions. Saves time and effort with quick, accurate matches powered by AI.
